Project Overview
Auren is a digital-first brand selling premium PDF guides focused on men's grooming, skincare, fashion, fragrance, and home skills. All products are delivered instantly via Shopify + Digital Downloads, targeting English-speaking men aged 20–40 in the United States.
Phase 1 Target Market: 🇺🇸 United States  
Phase 2 Target Market: 🌎 LATAM (Spanish versions — coming soon)  
Primary Format: Downloadable PDF Guides  
Sales Platform: Shopify  
Content Language: English

Full Customer Journey
```

         ↓
Clicks link → Lands on Landing Page (GitHub/Netlify)
         ↓
Flowise AI Agent activates 🤖
"Hey! What are you looking to improve today?"
         ↓
User picks: Style / Skincare / Home Skills / Fragrance
         ↓
Agent recommends specific PDF or Bundle
         ↓
"Get it here →" button links to Shopify product page
         ↓

         ↓

         ↓
```

Influencer Strategy
Target Profile
Male lifestyle, grooming, fashion, or fitness content creators
English-speaking audience, US-based preferred
5K–100K followers (nano/micro tier = best ROI)
Platforms: Instagram, TikTok, YouTube Shorts
Outreach Process
```
1. Find influencer in target niche
2. DM or email with personalized pitch
3. Offer: Free PDF + 15-20% commission on every sale
4. Provide unique Bitly tracking link
5. Agree on: minimum 1 post + 1 story or reel
6. Track clicks and conversions via Bitly dashboard
7. Pay commissions monthly via PayPal or Venmo
```
Pitch Template (English)

AI Agent — Flowise
Purpose
A conversational mini-agent embedded in the landing page that:
Greets visitors in English
Asks what they want to improve (style / skin / fragrance / home)
Recommends the right PDF or bundle
Provides a direct link to the Shopify product page
Setup Stack
Flowise (open source, self-hosted)
Hosted on: Render.com (free tier for pilot)
Model: Claude or OpenAI GPT (via API)
Embedded via: Flowise chat widget (iframe or JS snippet in landing page)
Sample Agent Flow
```
Agent: "Hey! Welcome to Auren 👋 What are you looking to level up?"

Options:
  A) My style & wardrobe
  B) My skincare routine
  C) Colognes & fragrances
  D) Home & life skills

[User picks B]

Agent: "Nice — taking care of your skin is one of the highest-ROI
things you can do. We have two guides for you:

🧴 The Skin Fix — Full skincare routine from scratch → $12
🫧 The Face Routine — Daily & weekly step-by-step system → $12

Or grab both for $19 with The Grooming Starter.

Ready to grab yours? → [Shop Now]"
```

Risks & Mitigations
Risk	Likelihood	Impact	Mitigation
PDFs perceived as low value	Medium	High	Strong visual design, clear outcome-based titles
PDF piracy / sharing	High	Medium	Watermark with buyer's email, focus on volume
Influencers don't post consistently	High	Medium	Written agreement, track via Bitly
Flowise setup takes longer than expected	Medium	Medium	Launch landing page first without agent, add later
Content feels generic (AI-generated)	Medium	High	Edit every PDF with a human voice before publishing
No sales in Month 1	Low-Medium	Medium	Expectation set: Month 1 = validation, not profit
